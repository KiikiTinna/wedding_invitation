Wedding Day Protocol

Dear Friends,

The main celebration will take place in the evening at 6:30 PM at Glamur Garden Restaurant in Bitola. This is where we would be most happy to see you!

However, if you wish to experience the full traditional Macedonian wedding day, you are more than welcome to join us for the earlier celebrations. Below you will find the complete schedule for both the groom's and bride's sides.

Please note that attending the earlier events is entirely optional. We will be equally delighted to celebrate with you at the evening reception.

If you would like to join us for the earlier celebrations, please let us know via our RSVP form.

Groom's Side Protocol ♥
Morning Preparation Optional
8:00 AM - 10:30 AM
The groom and his family prepare for the day. Traditional music, dancing, and celebrating with close family members. Location: Groom's appartment in Krushevo (Villa La Munte).
Departure to Bride's Home Optional
10:30 AM - 10:45 AM
The wedding party travels to the bride's home with music and celebration. (Approximately a 10-minute walk.)
Ceremony at Bride's Home Optional
11:00 AM - 12:00 PM
Traditional ceremonies at the bride's family home including the fetching of the bride, symbolic rituals, and celebrations with both families.
Church Ceremony Optional
12:30 PM - 13:30 PM
Religious ceremony at the church. Location: The Church of Bogorodica, Krushevo (Approximately a 10-minute walk.)
Evening Reception Main Event
6:30 PM onwards
This is the main celebration! Cocktail hour, dinner, traditional dances, music, and celebration at Glamur Garden Restaurant, Bitola. We would be most happy to see you here!
Bride's Side Protocol ♥
Morning Preparation Optional
8:00 AM - 11:00 AM
The bride gets ready with her family and close friends. Music , food, dancing, taking photos. Location: Bride's family home (Tome Nikle 49 Krushevo).
Welcoming the Groom Optional
11:00 AM - 12:00 PM
The groom's party arrives and traditional ceremonies take place. Symbolic customs, dancing, and celebration before the bride leaves her family home.
Church Ceremony Optional
12:30 PM - 13:30 PM
Religious ceremony at the church. Location: The Church of Bogorodica, Krushevo (Approximately a 10-minute walk.)
Evening Reception Main Event
6:30 PM onwards
This is the main celebration! Cocktail hour, dinner, traditional dances, music, and celebration at Glamur Garden Restaurant, Bitola. We would be most happy to see you here!
